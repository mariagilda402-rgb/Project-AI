# ⚕️ THE RESONANCE MODEL
### from concept to perceptual lens for ai

<br>

### ════════════════════════════════════════════
### PART 1: THE FOUNDATION
### ════════════════════════════════════════════

<pre>
  Puzzles      → every piece of info is a puzzle with unique edges
  Edges        → they connect via lock-and-key alignment
  Open Loops   → a question creates space for a match
  Anchors      → strategic concepts linking different regions
  Erosion      → overused edges lose their precision
  Resonance    → multiple connections at a single point
    Clarity    → the quantity of connections
    Color      → the diversity of connections
</pre>

### ════════════════════════════════════════════
### PART 2: WHAT EMERGES
### ════════════════════════════════════════════

### Take any element from Part 1. 
### Apply the rest of the elements to it. 
### Then take the next one. Apply the rest. 
### Every time, you see the same pattern:

<pre>
  +1 appears → it fits or it doesn't → what fits creates a new +1
</pre>

### Three lines. The list in Part 1 is what you see 
### when looking at them from different perspectives.

### ════════════════════════════════════════════
### PART 3: TERNARY
### ════════════════════════════════════════════

<pre>
  +1  →  fits, creates a new connection
   0  →  waiting, yet to be determined
  -1  →  doesn't fit, falls away
</pre>

### We didn't add this. It was there from the beginning.

### ════════════════════════════════════════════
### PART 4: DIRECTIONS
### ════════════════════════════════════════════

<pre>
  Upward      →  what FOLLOWS from this? (emergence)

</pre>
